In Memory of Radhe

Support the family of a dedicated wildlife conservationist who lost his life protecting the Thar Desert's wildlife.

Why Your Support Matters

Radheshyam Bishnoi, a passionate 28-year-old wildlife conservationist from the Bishnoi community, tragically lost his life on May 24, 2023. He died in a road accident while en route to prevent deer poaching in the Thar Desert. Three other conservationists also lost their lives in this devastating accident.

Throughout his short but impactful life, Radheshyam dedicated himself to protecting endangered species, particularly the critically endangered Great Indian Bustard. He built water reservoirs for wildlife, rescued injured animals, and tirelessly fought against poaching in the desert region.

Your generous contribution will directly support Radheshyam's family during this extremely difficult time. By donating, you help honor the legacy of a remarkable conservationist who gave his life protecting India's precious wildlife.
